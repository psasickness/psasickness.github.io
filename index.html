Sickness and How to Avoid it

How can you get infected?

Direct Contact

◉ Through a bite (rabies, etc.)

◉ Through exchange of body fluid (from sexual intercourse or sharing of a needle)

◉ Through birth (mother can transfer disease in the womb)

Indirect Contact

◉ Through surfaces (table, chair, etc.)

◉ Through the air (from coughs or sneezes)

Other

◉ Vectors (bugs can contaminate with carried sicknesses)

◉ Food

How to avoid getting sick

◉ Keep clean (wash hands often)

◉ Keep hands away from your face

◉ Acoid contact/exposure

◉ Be aware of your actions

◉ Be knowledgeable

What are pathogens?

Pathogens are a bacterium, virus, or other microorganism that can cause disease
